Hello, there!

My name is Kunal Nagar and welcome to my blog.

After completing my graduation from Pune Institute of Computer Technology in 2010, I was introduced to Apex Salesforce and worked on it for a year in Jaipur. I then decided to do my Masters from BITS Dubai in 2011 where I got the opportunity to work on college Intranet Projects using PHP and entered the world of freelancing.

In 2015, I got an offer to work for a social media startup called Viraltag, so I packed my bags and moved to Bangalore. Since then, I'm a full-time Frontend Developer. I had worked on AngularJS before during my Freelance days and at Viraltag, I got a chance to work on Backbone.js, Marionette.js and React.

I currently work at ScaleGrid Inc where I build experiences on the web that allow users to manage and host their databases MongoDB® & Redis®.

If you wish to contact me, don't hesitate to send an email to me@kunalnagar.in.

You can also find me on Twitter, GitHub and LinkedIn.
